About Us

BMW has launched the new generation of the 7 Series sedan in India at a whopping price of Rs 1.70 crore (ex-showroom). The German marque has also introduced an all-electric version of the flagship limousine in the country—the i7 with a sticker price of Rs 1.95 crore (ex-showroom). Offered in a single, fully-loaded 740i variant, the new 7 Series is now available as a petrol-powered limousine only. Its all-electric sibling is also offered in a single i7 xDrive 60 trim .Deliveries of both these models, which made their global debut in April last year are expected to commence from March 2023 onwards.
